# Axioms of LBTC Ecosystem

These are immutable principles that govern all systems built on this ontology.

---

## Axiom 1: Identity & Immutability

**Statement:** Every Block created within the system possesses a unique, cryptographically verifiable identity that remains eternally immutable and globally unique across all temporal and spatial domains.

**Implication:**
- Historical records cannot be retrofitted or reinterpreted
- All modifications create new Blocks with explicit lineage
- Audit trails are complete and permanent
- No data erasure, only archival or deprecation

**Application:**
- Medical records remain queryable indefinitely
- Transactions are permanent ledger entries
- Smart contract execution history is immutable
- Business decisions are permanently recorded

---

## Axiom 2: Semantic Determinism

**Statement:** The transformation of raw data into semantic structures follows deterministic, non-probabilistic transformation rules. Given identical input and Semantics, the system must produce identical outputs without variance or interpretation drift.

**Implication:**
- All Semantics rules must be mathematically precise
- No heuristics or statistical approximations in core transformations
- Reversibility or explicit information loss must be documented
- Future systems can reproduce all historical transformations

**Application:**
- Medical codes are deterministically mapped across regions
- Currency conversions are exactly reproducible
- Smart contract execution is deterministic
- Business processes are repeatably automatable

---

## Axiom 3: Structural Isolation & Polyglot Compatibility

**Statement:** The system maintains complete logical independence between data structure (Table) and interpretation rules (Semantics). No implicit conversion, schema coercion, or lossy translation occurs at boundaries.

**Implication:**
- Tables remain independently queryable
- Legacy data remains valid despite evolving Semantics
- Different implementations can coexist without mutual dependency
- Future storage paradigms integrate transparently

**Application:**
- Medical data in any format remains accessible
- Wallets can store any currency type
- Blockchains remain backward-compatible
- Business processes transcend implementation details

---

## Axiom 4: Distributed Consensus

**Statement:** No single Actor, Node, or system has authoritative control over the state. Consensus is achieved through distributed agreement mechanisms that are verifiable and fault-tolerant.

**Implication:**
- Central points of failure are eliminated
- All decisions are collectively verifiable
- Malicious actors cannot unilaterally corrupt state
- Agreement is auditable and reproducible

**Application:**
- Medical data validation requires independent verification
- Wallet transactions need distributed confirmation
- Blockchain decisions require network consensus
- Business approvals need multiple stakeholders

---

## Axiom 5: Actor Sovereignty & Auditability

**Statement:** Every Operation carries explicit Actor identity, permissions, and audit trail. Actors maintain sovereign control over their Accounts while remaining subject to Consensus rules.

**Implication:**
- All actions are attributable
- Actors cannot disavow their Operations
- Permissions are explicit and granular
- Audit trails enable full reconstruction of events

**Application:**
- Doctors are accountable for diagnoses
- Users control their wallets
- Traders are responsible for their trades
- Managers own their decisions

---

## Axiom 6: Temporal Coherence

**Statement:** The system maintains a consistent temporal ordering of all Events. Each Block carries timestamp and causal ordering that prevents temporal paradoxes.

**Implication:**
- Causality is preserved
- Time travel in semantics is impossible
- Historical reconstruction is deterministic
- Timeline branches are explicit and auditable

**Application:**
- Medical history follows patient timeline
- Transaction history is chronologically consistent
- Smart contract execution follows block ordering
- Business timeline is reconstructible

---

## Axiom 7: Semantic Pluralism

**Statement:** Multiple competing Semantics can coexist for the same data. The system provides mechanisms for translation and mapping without privileging any single interpretation.

**Implication:**
- Different domains have legitimate interpretations
- Medical systems can use different taxonomies
- Currencies have different value systems
- Business processes vary by region and culture
- No single "right" interpretation is enforced

**Application:**
- Medical records support multiple diagnostic frameworks
- Wallets support multiple currency codes
- Smart contracts support multiple execution models
- Business processes adapt to local regulations

---

## Derived Principles

### From Axiom 1 + Axiom 2:
**Historical Reproducibility:** Any historical state can be exactly reproduced by applying Semantics to historical Blocks.

### From Axiom 2 + Axiom 3:
**Schema Evolution:** Tables can evolve without invalidating existing data, as long as Semantics remain deterministic.

### From Axiom 4 + Axiom 5:
**Distributed Accountability:** Actors are individually accountable while participating in collective Consensus.

### From Axiom 5 + Axiom 6:
**Causal Responsibility:** Each Actor is responsible only for Operations within their causal cone.

### From Axiom 6 + Axiom 7:
**Multi-Temporal Semantics:** Different time periods can have different interpretation rules, as long as mappings are explicit.
